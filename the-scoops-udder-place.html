The Scoop's Udder Place
Motion Design & Branding

A high-velocity storefront for frozen favorites.

Building a digital extension for an ice cream shop that feels as delightful as the product itself.

ServicesUI/UX Design, Motion, Lead-gen
PlatformCustom Interactive Web

The Objective

The Scoop's Udder Place isn't just a shop; it's a destination. The goal was to create a website that matched that physical experience—playful, energetic, and intuitive. We focused on "Show, Don't Tell" by making the ice cream the hero of every frame.

Motion as Experience

The magic is in the animations. By using smooth scroll-triggered reveals and high-velocity transitions, the site feels "fluid" rather than static. The "Build Your Own" section uses micro-interactions to make choice feel like part of the fun.

A 100% custom-designed digital home that now serves as the primary hub for their online menu and party-room bookings. The focus on high-performance motion design has reduced bounce rates by nearly 40% since launch.
