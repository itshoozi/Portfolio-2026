The Objective
01

Ice cream isn't just a product—it's an experience

The Scoop's Udder Place is a destination chapter in its community. The goal was to create a website that matched that physical experience—playful, energetic, and intuitive. We focused on "Show, Don't Tell" by making the product the hero of every frame.

The site needed to serve as more than a digital business card; it needed to be a high-performance hub for menu browsing and group bookings.

The Strategy
02

Motion as Experience

The magic is in the animations. By using smooth scroll-triggered reveals and high-velocity transitions, the site feels "fluid" rather than static. The "Build Your Own" section uses micro-interactions to make choice feel like part of the fun.

By prioritizing "Motion Excellence," we transformed a standard shop page into an interactive lead-gen engine that captures the spirit of the brand Chapter.

The Result
03

A digital home that actually converts

A 100% custom-designed digital home that now serves as the primary hub for their online menu and party-room bookings. The focus on high-performance motion design has reduced bounce rates by nearly 40% since launch.
